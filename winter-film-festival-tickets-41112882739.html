Event description

Description

Join Friends of Blackwater for an evening of films and fun at White Grass Ski Center!

Beat the winter blues with short films from the Winter Wildland Alliance celebrating outdoor recreation and other snowy adventures. There will be a pasta buffet available for dinner from the White Grass kitchen ($12, not included in ticket price) and a fun raffle featuring items donated by local businesses. Doors open at 5:30 but the film screening will not begin right away, in order to give everyone time to chat, get their dinner, and buy raffle tickets.

Directions:

If you are coming from Davis, take Rte. 32 past Timberline resort, and take a left onto Freeland Rd. After a litte over a mile, turn right onto Weiss Knob Ski Rd. at the sign for White Grass. Keep right into the parking lot.

If you are coming from Harman, take 32 into Canaan Valley, past the Canaan Valley Resort. Take a right onto Freeland Rd. After a little over a mile, turn right onto Weiss Knob Ski Rd. at the sign for White Grass. Keep right into the parking lot.
