2010 - present

March 18th 2010: Gas Leases near Spruce Knob Protested and Stopped.

April 8th 2010: Sent Notice letter to WVDNR and US Fish and Wildlife Service on threats to Cheat Snail and Indiana bat from logging at Coopers Rock State Forest.

June 6th 2010: Notice letter on Logging in Blackwater Canyon sent to AWP and US Fish and Wildlife Service.

July 26th 2010: US Forest Service released Record of Decision< denying AWP's request for an easement to build a logging haul road along the Blackwater Canyon Trail.

March 25, 2011 - WV Northern Flying Squirrel's protected Endangered Status was reinstated.

June 2011 - Over 1000 Letters to Senator Manchin requesting National Park Study.

July 2011 - Senator Manchin made a formal request to National Park Service for a Reconnaissance Study in WV.

Nov 2011 - NPS announced that they will begin a Reconnaissance Study in the High Allegheny region of West Virginia.
