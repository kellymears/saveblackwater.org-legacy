Tell the WV State Board of Education No to Compromising Science Education!

West Virginia science teachers came toBlackwater Falls last June for our Climate Change and the highlands Conference, and they showed they understand the threats to our precious mountain ecology and economy from a changing climate. 

We are so proud of our teachers for telling the State Board of Education this month that they insist on teaching real science on climate change.  We were proud to help them this month in Charleston, as the board withdrew proposed standards that cast doubt on the reality of climate change.

Learn more about what we did and see media coverage at www.alleghenyclimate.org.  It was a big win for climate-smart thinking in West Virginia!

Now we have to make this commitment to real science permanent, and help our teachers do their jobs!  Click here to send a message to the West Virginia Department of Education telling them that good science is good for everyone!  Your voice is important!  Please send your message now!
