Welcome to the website of Friends of Blackwater's Allegheny Highlands Climate Change Impacts Initiative.

We are revising and updating this site, and most of our buttons/sections/internal links may not be completely up to date. We hope to complete this revision and update soon and we apologize for any inconvenience.

In the meantime, we are proud to present an updated report (August 2016) and new scientific bibliography on the impacts of climate change and global warming on the Allegheny Highlands. 

The report and bibliography is titled "On the Chopping Block" and a PDF version can be found here.  Feel free to download and share.  We welcome your comments and suggestions; send them here.

You can also get on our e-update list by clicking here to be notified of future changes to this site and future Initiative activities.  We do not share your name or personal information.

Finally, we regularly post new scientific studies and other news at our Facebook page, which is here.
Thanks for your interest!
