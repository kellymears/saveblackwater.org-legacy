This guide is meant to educate citizens to understand the negative impacts of fracking and to be a resource for challenging and evaluating oil and gas extraction, and hydrofracking, on our national forests. It was created through a collaboration of many environmental groups and individuals.
Please contact us with any questions, information and additional documents to be shared with others.
Special thanks to The Colcom Foundation for their generous support. Special thanks to the Eastern National Forests Oil & Gas Working Group for their participation.
The information contained in this document is based on a guide written in 2002; readers should verify information is current and has not changed before relying on it.
