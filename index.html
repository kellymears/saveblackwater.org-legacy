NEWS

West Virginia and the Allegheny Highlands are home to many beloved creatures who depend on the Endangered Species Act for protection.  But now the Trump administration wants to gut the Act! 

“Ginny,” the amazing WV Northern Flying Squirrel; the cute-as-a-button Cheat Mountain salamander , Lucy the northern long-eared bat  -- and all their pals in our streams, rivers, and forests -- are counting on us to speak out for the natural world.  We have to stop this assault on our basic environmental protection laws.  We must tell our members of Congress that the Endangered Species Act is doing a good job, and we need it more than ever. 

Please let your voice be heard!  Fill out the ESA support letter here, click submit and it will be sent to West Virginia Senator Joe Manchin

Remember, extinction is forever! 

Thanks, Judy Rodd, Director, Friends of Blackwater

PS - It will help if you add a sentence about your love of West Virginia’s rare wildlife.
